Box League - Mixed Singles Matches

For all members over 16.

Players will be grouped into boxes (typically 4–5 players per group), and each player plays every other player in their box during the round.

Round Periods

Round Period
Round 1February – March
Round 2April – May
Round 3June – July
Round 4August – September
Round 5October – November

How It Works

  • Join Anytime: New players can join at the start of any round.
  • Box Grouping: Players will be grouped based on past performance or known ability.
  • Match Scheduling: Players contact each other directly to arrange matches.
  • Scoring: Best of 3 standard sets, OR 2 sets with a 10-point match tie break at 1 all.
  • Points: Win = 2 points, Loss = 1
  • Promotion/Relegation: Top players move up a box, bottom may move down (if enough participants).
  • Reporting Results: Post match scores using a google form: DTC Box League - Submit Scores - Google Forms
